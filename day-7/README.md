- Tachyon beam enters at location **`S`**
- Beams always move **downward**
- Beams pass freely through **empty space** (`.`)
- When a beam encounters a **splitter** (`^`):
  - The original beam **stops**
  - Two new beams emit from **immediate left** and **immediate right**

### Quantum Mechanics
With a quantum manifold:
- Only a **single tachyon particle** is sent through
- The particle takes **both left AND right** paths at each splitter
- This creates a **many-worlds** scenario

### Many-Worlds Interpretation
Each time a particle reaches a splitter, **time itself splits**:
- One timeline: particle went **left**
- Another timeline: particle went **right**

### The Question
How many **unique timelines** exist after the particle completes all possible journeys?
